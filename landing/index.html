Protocol Features

A comprehensive DeFi lending protocol with production-grade security and advanced financial primitives.

💰

Collateralized Lending

Deposit collateral and borrow against it with support for multiple Stellar asset types and dynamic interest rates.

⚡

Flash Loans

Borrow any amount without collateral within a single transaction. Perfect for arbitrage, liquidations, and collateral swaps.

Multisig support for critical parameter changes, social recovery, and upgrade mechanisms with rollback capability.

How It Works

Get started with StellarLend in three simple steps.

1

Deposit Collateral

Supply XLM or Stellar tokens as collateral to the protocol. Your assets are locked in auditable Soroban smart contracts.

2

Borrow Assets

Borrow against your collateral at dynamic interest rates. The protocol calculates your borrowing power in real time.

3

Repay & Earn

Repay your loan at any time. Lenders earn yield from borrower interest, distributed proportionally.
